About me

A few things
about me.

I don't usually start with an idea. I start with something that feels unnecessarily difficult — then I can't stop thinking about how to make it simpler.
I didn't choose design because I loved aesthetics. I chose it because I kept noticing things that felt harder than they should — and I couldn't stop imagining how they could work better. Design gave me a way to actually do something about it.
I usually start by asking two questions: who is this for, and what do they actually need? From there I research, sketch, design — and I build it. And if I don't know how yet, I learn — and then I do it. I keep testing until it works for the people it was made for, not just until it looks good. Accessibility is part of the conversation from the beginning.
I don't separate beautiful from functional — for me, good design is both. What drives me is building things that actually fit their purpose. Designs that work in real life and make someone's day a little easier without them even noticing.

You'll probably find me taking photos, watching soccer, organizing absolutely everything, or having mate with someone who loves a good conversation.

01 Break Board
● Live Designed Built Deployed
Live at ecomm-breaklog.web.app

My workplace had no system for tracking employee breaks — people lost track, managers had no visibility, and there was no history. I saw the problem and built the solution.

I designed the UI from scratch, coded it, and deployed it on Firebase. It's actively used by my team every day.

What it does

4 real-time break slots with countdowns, live occupancy counter, Kiosk Mode with PIN lock, manager History view, daily log with CSV export, and break extension requests.

Stack

HTML · CSS · JavaScript · Firebase (Hosting + Realtime Database)

02 Luquita
UX/UI Mobile App School Project

Luquita is a personal finance mobile app designed for young working adults — helping them manage their money, track goals, and build financial confidence.

Built as a school group project for Demo Day — the team designed a full product strategy alongside the UX: freemium model, ad monetization, and affiliate marketing. We weren't just designing screens, we were building a business.

My role

UX/UI design in Figma — UX research, user flows, wireframes, high-fidelity screens, and interactive prototype.

Problem

Young workers struggle to manage money without tools that match their lifestyle — existing apps are either too complex or too generic. Luquita meets them where they are.
